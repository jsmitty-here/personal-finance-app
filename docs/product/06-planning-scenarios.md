# Planning and What-If Scenarios

## Purpose

The app should help evaluate financial tradeoffs using household-specific data and configurable assumptions.

## Required Scenario Types

The app should support comparisons such as:

- Pay down loan vs invest
- Build emergency fund vs invest
- Mortgage prepayment vs brokerage investment
- Retirement contribution vs taxable investment
- Savings account vs debt payoff
- Debt payoff acceleration
- Investment growth projection
- Tax-aware income planning

## Scenario Inputs

Scenarios should support:

- Starting amount
- Monthly contribution
- Time horizon
- Expected investment return
- Loan interest rate
- Savings yield
- Tax rate
- Inflation assumption
- Risk preference
- Owner allocation

## Market and Rate Assumptions

The app should support market/rate assumptions from:

- User-entered assumptions
- Search/API-provided rates
- Historical market references
- Saved assumption profiles

## Output Requirements

Scenario outputs should include:

- Projected ending value
- Interest saved
- Interest earned
- Estimated tax impact
- Net benefit comparison
- Break-even point
- Risk/uncertainty notes

The app should not present projections as guaranteed outcomes.
