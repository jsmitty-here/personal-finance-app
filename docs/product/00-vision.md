# Personal Finance App Vision

## Purpose

This application helps a household understand, plan, and optimize personal finances across checking, savings, retirement, investment, loan, mortgage, and manually tracked accounts.

The app is designed first for personal use by a couple managing:
- Individual accounts
- Joint accounts
- Ownership-adjusted financial reporting
- Spending, saving, debt, tax, and investment decisions

This is not intended to be a commercial money movement product.

## Primary Goals

- Provide a unified view of household finances.
- Track net worth, spending, savings, investments, debts, taxes, and budgets.
- Support owner-based filtering and ownership-adjusted reporting.
- Make transaction categorization highly accurate and user-correctable.
- Enable what-if financial planning.
- Support local AI/LLM interaction with financial data.
- Keep the system extensible for future multi-user and multi-household use.

## Non-Goals

- Initiating transfers
- Paying bills
- Trading investments
- Acting as a bank
- Becoming a commercial finance platform
