# Product Scope

## In Scope

### Accounts

The app must support:

- Checking accounts
- Savings accounts
- Retirement accounts
- Brokerage / investment accounts
- Loan accounts
- Mortgage accounts
- Manually tracked assets
- Manually tracked liabilities

### Data Access

The app should support financial account aggregation through Plaid or a similar provider.

The app should support both:

- Current balances
- Transaction-level history

Manual account entry must also be supported for accounts that cannot be linked.

### Analysis

The app must support:

- Net worth tracking
- Spending trends
- Savings trends
- Investment trends
- Debt payoff tracking
- Budgeting
- Tax-related tracking
- Month-over-month spending
- Year-over-year spending
- Forecasting and planning scenarios

### AI

The app should support local LLM-based querying and analysis over financial data.

## Out of Scope

The app does not need to support:

- Moving money
- Paying bills
- Trading securities
- Commercial multi-tenant SaaS operation
